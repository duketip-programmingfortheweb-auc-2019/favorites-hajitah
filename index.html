Friends

Friends is one of the most iconic tv shows of all time. After being off the air for almost 14 years, it is still one of the most watched shows on Netflix. The five friends the show is based on have taken the hearts of millions of people in the world, and continue to do so everyday. The show follows brother and sister, Monica and Ross along with their friends, Chandler, his roommate Joey, and Pheobe across ten seasons through them being broke, getting married, and having fun.
